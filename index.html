The Bug Smashers

Anuj, Matt, Rugved, Jaren

Synopsis

eLearning Commons, the online educational tool, used by the University of Georgia is a branded license of the Desire2Learn, or d2L, education software. The text editor in specific has ceratin drawbacks, including anti-affordances and a lack of discoverability which is problematic for effective responses to questions. We analyze the flaws within the d2L text editor, compare several alternative solutions, and then discuss some of our own proposed solutions to this issue.
