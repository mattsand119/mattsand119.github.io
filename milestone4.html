The Bug Smashers

Anuj Patel, Matthew Sand, Rugved Gudapati, Jaren Spitters

Synopsis & Links

eLearning Commons, the online educational tool used by the University of Georgia, is a branded license of the Desire2Learn, or d2L, education software. The text editor in specific has certain drawbacks, including anti-affordances and a lack of discoverability which is problematic for effective responses to questions. In this milestone, we finish prototyping our designs, make demos for them, and comment on the testing process to determine the validity of our design decisions.
