The Bug Smashers

Anuj Patel, Matthew Sand, Rugved Gudapati, Jaren Spitters

Synopsis & Links

The eLC text editor has poor signifiers, and the process of adding images or files into the editor is complicated, requiring multiple steps. Our ideation process involved specifying the user stories based on our work in Milestone 2 and then brainstorming possible solutions to the user stories. After the ideation, we narrowed down the two best solutions for each user story and then created low fidelity wireframe mock-ups. After we had two low fidelity wireframes for each user story, we picked one best solution for each and created high fidelity wireframes for each of the best solutions.
